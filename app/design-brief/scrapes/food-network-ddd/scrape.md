# Scrape Results: Food Network DDD
URL: https://www.foodnetwork.com/shows/diners-drive-ins-and-dives

## Site Focus
Official branding (colors, typography, imagery style), episode card layout.

## UX Patterns

### 1. Branding & Visuals
- Color Palette: Red (Primary), Black, White. Very "official" and polished.
- High-quality photography for restaurant and food shots.
- Official DDD Logo is prominent.

### 2. Navigation
- Mega-menu for "Recipes", "Shows", "Chefs", etc.
- Breadcrumbs for navigation context.
- Persistent search bar in the header.

### 3. Content Sections
- "The Latest": Grid of gallery/article cards with labels (Gallery, Article).
- "About The Show": Brief description and schedule.
- "About the Host": Feature section for Guy Fieri with links to his social media.
- "Schedule": List of upcoming episodes with "Recently on TV" tag.
- "Episodes": Season selector and episode list with descriptions and "View Recipes" links.

### 4. Card Layouts
- Gallery cards show a large image with a title overlay or caption.
- Episode cards show a thumbnail, season/episode number, title, and a short summary.

### 5. Mobile Responsiveness
- Nav menu collapses into a simple hamburger.
- Grid layouts stack vertically.
- Content remains clear but can be long (needs scrolling).
